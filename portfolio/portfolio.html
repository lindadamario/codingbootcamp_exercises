About Me

I would like to express my interest for the position of “Project Coordinator”. I am a graduating student in HR Management with a strong knowledge background in Training and Developmental programs for companies. Moreover, thanks to the many work experiences I did, I have been able to gain skills in various areas such as event management, office management, recruitment and crosscultural communication. This helped me to comprehend in-depth the HR management and development under multiple perspectives. What encouraged me to apply for Claned is my passion in creating a unique and easy-going work-environment in order to make the employees appreciated and valued. Confucius said “Choose a job you love and you will never work a day in your life”. My choice was HR and I want to specialize in this area All these accomplishments would not have been possible without the support of my supervisor and colleagues, who helped me during my training with a non-judgemental attitude but, to the contrary, with a mentoring attitude. They would describe me as a social person who “gets-things-done” precise and fast and as a team member always ready to have constructive discussion in order to grow professionally and personally. I am sure they would remark my strong motivation in carrying out the tasks and my will to be an HR professional.
